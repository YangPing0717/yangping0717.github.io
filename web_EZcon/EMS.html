EZ-EMS Energy
Management System

Unlike general companies that only perform EMS energy management from charging piles, Yikong performs energy management from the source of community power boxes. Therefore, there is no need to bind charging piles, and car owners can choose original or other brand charging piles at will.

There are two solutions in total. The first is to use the wireless meter of Schneider , a major European manufacturer, to detect power usage. The wireless meter is compact and does not take up space in the low-voltage box, and it looks beautiful. The second method is to use the architecture of the lead-sealed meter for control. Both have their advantages and disadvantages, and the choice mainly depends on what the community cares about.


EasyControl provides a Community EMS Proposal for residents, chairpersons, car owners, etc. to reference and provides Community Management Methods for the management committee to reference.

EasyControl EMS
is different other brands

After reading the two architectures, you want to understand the difference between
the EasyControl EMS system and other brands of EMS?
What are the advantages for administrators and tram owners?

To the Management Committee
  • The Easy-Control EMS system is also integrated with the property management system. Managers can directly submit car owners' charging costs to their personal monthly management fees with one click, without having to export to Excel or calculate one by one.
  • Buyout EMS system, no monthly rental fee required.
  • The backend of the EMS system can be flexibly adjusted according to government policies or changes in the management committee.
For Users
  • The system uses a local billing host and does not transmit information through the cloud. Even if the network is disconnected, there is no need to worry about the loss of electricity usage data or the risk of information security being leaked.
  • Not bound to charging piles, car owners can buy original or other brands of charging piles. The system supports Tesla original charging piles and OCPP protocol charging piles.
  • Integrated payment service, users can use the App to scan the QR code to pay at convenience stores. It is paperless and environmentally friendly, and detailed records can be viewed on the App.
  • Integrate financial flow services, and the fees paid by users will go directly into the designated account of the management committee without going through the system provider's account. There is no need to worry about being charged usage fees or electricity bills.
