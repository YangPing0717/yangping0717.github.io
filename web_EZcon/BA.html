People-oriented, close to residential use needs. Enhance the interaction between people and buildings through technology. Meet practical needs with innovative thinking.

Easy Control Wisdom has complete solutions and customized services for smart building systems.

Easy Control is committed to the research and development of AIoT. In order to provide excellent experience for building management and residents, we independently developed and integrated the eHome system, smart building system, and community property software to connect the three together to provide overall solutions for building management, residents, and construction projects. The systems can also operate independently and complement each other, making building intelligence more humane.

Why do we need building management?

Building maintenance costs account for 82.5% of the total cost. Therefore, during the operation and maintenance period, costs should be reduced and operational efficiency should be improved as much as possible. Easy-to-control intelligence improves the software integration and usability of smart building systems and solves many pain points in building management!
