Guanxi Meiju Jingsen is located in Guanxi Town, Hsinchu County. It is a smart residential project completed by Meiju Construction in 2019. The project integrates technology and design, emphasizing the practicality, safety and functionality of the home environment, providing residents with a diversified smart living experience.

Smart home integration system

Dimming system
Use American Lutron dimmers and German Jung switches to achieve precise lighting control.
Access control intercom system
equipped with smart door locks, human body sensors, touch screens and intercoms to enhance security and convenience.
Automation system
including aquaponics system, electric curtains and audio-visual equipment to improve the level of home automation.
Air conditioning system
Integrates air conditioning and a four-in-one heater to provide a comfortable indoor environment.

Safety sensing system
Equipped with gas detectors, shut-off devices and multi-function sensors to ensure home safety.
Industrial style design
The ceiling is configured with EMT metal exposed pipes, showing the unique beauty of industrial style, while also having the advantages of anti-interference and high security.
Urban Farm - Fish-vegetable Symbiosis System
Create your own small farm at home, provide nutrients to plants through a simple water circulation system, and achieve a green life without the need for fertilization and care.
