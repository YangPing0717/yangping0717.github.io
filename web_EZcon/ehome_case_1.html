Smart Office for Corporate Headquarters Case

by Chief Executive Officer Benny ZHOU
  • SmartOffice
  • E-Home
  • SmartBuilding

The entrance is combined with facial recognition access control, and visitors can make appointments for visits. The entire area uses a smart environmental control system | Tour mode, meeting mode, briefing mode, commuting mode. Make corresponding adjustments to lighting, curtains, and audio-visual systems. The interior of the smart office in Xitun District, Taichung City, showcases an advanced smart environmental control system, providing users with an intuitive experience.

Access Control System

A facial recognition access control system is used at the entrance, combined with a visitor reservation function to enhance security and convenience.

Intelligent environmental control system

The entire office area is equipped with an intelligent environmental control system, providing multiple modes
Guided tour mode
suitable for leading visitors on a tour. The system automatically adjusts the lights and curtains to create a comfortable guided tour environment.
Meeting mode
When activated, lights, curtains, and audio-visual equipment automatically adjust to create a professional meeting atmosphere.
Presentation Mode
The system automatically closes the curtains, adjusts the lights and starts the projection equipment to facilitate briefings.
Commute mode
Automatically control lighting, air conditioning and other equipment according to commuting time, which is energy-saving and efficient.

Comprehensive solutions for smart buildings

The office also integrates a variety of smart building solutions, including
Management System
It provides property management front-end and back-end functions, health measurement, unmanned package collection display, emergency rescue linkage equipment, charging and billing auxiliary system, public broadcast system and smart elevator calling system.
BA system
Includes earthquake linkage system, human/facial recognition system, various detectors, surveillance integration, security intercom system and energy management dashboard.

Green Energy Application

In order to achieve energy recycling, solar panels and energy storage cabinets were installed in the office to use clean energy to reduce peak loads and fill valleys, thereby achieving the goal of energy conservation and carbon reduction. The design of the smart office in Xitun District, Taichung City combines modern technology with humanistic care, providing users with an efficient, comfortable and environmentally friendly working environment.
