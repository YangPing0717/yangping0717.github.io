Yongfeng Shangyu is located in Sanchong, New Taipei City, and is developed by Yongfeng Construction, with a total of 119 households. The project has been awarded the Silver Level Certification of the Smart Building Label and adopts the one-stop service of Easy-Control Smart Living Technology, covering everything from consulting, planning and design to integrated construction and subsequent maintenance, comprehensively improving the smart living experience of residents.

Easy Control Smart Building One-Stop Service

Label Consultancy Service 
The label consultancy department of Easy Control participated in the whole process of this project, assisted the project to meet the requirements of the Smart Building Label, provided professional consultation from the initial planning to the verification process, and ensured that this project successfully obtained the Smart Building Label Silver Certification.

Planning and construction of smart weak current system 
BA system 
Integrates monitoring, access control, parking management, environmental detection and other functions to enable automated building management and improve efficiency and safety.
SIP intercom system 
improves the communication efficiency between residents and the management center and ensures smooth communication within the community.
Innovative smart home system 
The application of the Easy-Control eHome system in Yongfeng Shangyu allows residents to experience a truly smart home life 
Standard electronic door locks enhance security and convenience. Smart home appliance control, including lighting, air conditioning, curtains and other situational modes, can be easily managed through mobile phone or voice.

Smart property management
Health Care Station

Smart charging and billing system
