Zhaojiele Community is located in Taiwan and is expected to be completed in 2023. The project adopts the solution of Easy-Control Smart Life Technology to provide residents with advanced smart home experience.

Easy-Control System Main features

Smart home integration system
Including dimming system, lighting system, smart curtain system and air conditioning system. Residents can easily manage various devices in their home through a centralized control panel or mobile phone application to improve the convenience of life.

Security protection
Equipped with advanced access control system and video surveillance equipment to ensure the safety of residents at home.
Environmental monitoring
Built-in air quality detector monitors indoor air quality in real time and works with the air conditioning system to provide a healthy and comfortable living environment.

Through the integration of these smart systems, Zhaojie Le Community has created a safe, comfortable and convenient modern living space for residents.
