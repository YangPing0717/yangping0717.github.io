Dingtai Zhenhuan Sales Center Sample House is Dingtai Construction’s landmark smart residential project in Taoyuan and will be completed in 2022. Each household in this project is equipped with an eHome system, which is connected with property management and building BA (Building Automation) applications to create Taiwan's most advanced AIoT smart community.

e-Home Main System Configuration

Security System
Provide all-round security protection to ensure residents can live in peace.
Access Intercom System
Advanced access control and intercom equipment is used to facilitate communication between residents and visitors.
Monitoring system
Surveillance equipment is deployed throughout the community to monitor public areas in real time and improve safety.
Environmental data analysis
Environmental data is collected through sensors and analyzed to optimize the living environment.
Audiovisual equipment control
Centrally manage home audio-visual devices to enhance entertainment experience.

Lighting dimming control
Residents can adjust the brightness of indoor lights according to their needs to create an ideal atmosphere.
Audiovisual equipment control
Centrally manage home audio-visual devices to enhance entertainment experience.
Air conditioning system control
Intelligently adjust the indoor temperature to provide a comfortable living environment.
Electric curtain control
The opening and closing of curtains can be controlled through the system, which is convenient and energy-saving.

Through the integration of the above systems, Dingtai Zhenhuan has created a safe, comfortable and intelligent living environment for residents, fully demonstrating the charm of modern smart homes.
